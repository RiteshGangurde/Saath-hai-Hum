About Saathaihum Placement

The Saathaihum Placement Program by SaathaiHum Foundation bridges the gap between talented youth, academic institutions, and global recruiters. We empower students, fresh graduates, and unemployed youth by connecting them to verified national and international opportunities.

Career building is more than job postings — it’s about meaningful connections between industry needs and individual potential. Our platform enables students to apply directly, colleges to monitor placements, and recruiters to manage opportunities efficiently.

By streamlining the process, we save time, increase placement rates, and create pathways that transform education into employment, and employment into empowerment.

How It Works

Simple, transparent steps to connect talent with opportunity.

For Students & Youth

  • Register with your details and resume.
  • Log in to view and apply for jobs.

For Colleges & Universities

  • Register your institution to access job postings.
  • Share verified opportunities with students.

For Companies & Recruiters

  • Register to post job ads and connect with talent.
