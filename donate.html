Donation

Overview

At SaathaiHum Foundation, every contribution you make helps us take one step closer to our vision — bridging education, research, industry, and community development to create a stronger and more inclusive society.

Your donation directly supports our initiatives in:
• Promoting online & offline higher education
• Funding student research & innovation
• Providing placement & entrepreneurship support
• Empowering women and rural communities
• Promoting healthcare, sustainability, and environmental conservation
• Building partnerships for nation-building and social impact

Education & Research

Scholarships, online learning platforms, and research funding opportunities to empower learners and innovators worldwide.

Community Development

Women empowerment programs, rural development projects, and accessible healthcare services for stronger communities.

Innovation & Employment

Seed funding for startups, entrepreneurship mentoring, and placement support for job seekers and innovators.

Why Your Support Matters

From funding scholarships for deserving students to supporting academic–industry collaborations, your generosity fuels programs that change lives. Every rupee is invested in creating measurable, lasting impact through our core services:

• Admissions assistance
• Awards & recognition programs
• Research and entrepreneurship support
• Academic–industry immersion
• Placement and career development
• Seed funding for startups and innovations

Tax Benefits – 80G Exemption

SaathaiHum Foundation is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, allowing donors to claim tax exemption on their contributions. Your support not only creates impact but also offers you financial benefits. A donation receipt will be issued for your records and tax claim.
