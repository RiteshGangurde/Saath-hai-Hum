About Saathaihum Foundation (SAHFON)

Saathaihum Foundation (SAHFON) is a Section 8 non-profit organization committed to promoting education, innovation, and social development across India and beyond. Established with the vision of bridging the gap between academia, industry, and community, we work collaboratively with educational institutions, industries, government bodies, and NGOs to create lasting impact. Our focus areas include higher education promotion, research support, skill development, and entrepreneurship mentoring. We actively work with schools, colleges, universities, and research centers to enhance education standards and foster innovation. Through seed funding, research grants, and intellectual property protection, we empower students, faculty, and innovators to transform ideas into reality.

Beyond academics, SAHFON is dedicated to community welfare and sustainable development. We collaborate with industries on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives and grants, supporting programs in healthcare, environmental conservation, and women’s empowerment. Special emphasis is placed on uplifting rural communities and providing training and resources for women entrepreneurs to achieve financial independence. We believe in academic–industry immersion, offering internships, live projects, and placement support to equip students with real-world skills. By partnering with organizations nationwide, we facilitate capacity building, career guidance, and social awareness campaigns that drive positive change. At Saathaihum Foundation, partnerships are at the heart of everything we do. Guided by our mission — Support to Success — we strive to empower individuals and institutions, fostering a stronger, more inclusive future where education, innovation, and social progress go hand in hand.
