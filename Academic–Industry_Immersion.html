Program Overview

The SaatHai Hum Academic & Industry Immersion Program bridges the gap between theory and real-world application. This national initiative connects academic institutions with experienced industry professionals for guest lectures, mentoring, workshops, and knowledge-sharing sessions.

Students gain practical exposure, understand market demands, and are prepared with essential career skills. Beyond career readiness, the program fosters social responsibility, inspiring students to use their skills as tools for societal progress.

Key Objectives

  • Facilitate Industry–Academia Collaboration – Building partnerships that strengthen both educational quality and workforce readiness.
  • Encourage Practical Learning – Equipping students with industry-aligned skills and knowledge.
  • Promote Knowledge Transfer & Mentorship – Inspiring students through direct interaction with accomplished professionals.

Program Activities

  • Guest Lectures by subject matter experts
  • Skill Development Workshops tailored to industry trends
  • Mentorship Programs connecting students to career guidance
  • Panel Discussions & Webinars for knowledge sharing
  • Industry Exposure Visits for hands-on experience

Who Can Participate

  • Colleges & Universities seeking to enhance academic offerings
  • Industry Professionals wishing to share expertise and mentor young talent
  • Corporate Partners supporting talent development initiatives
